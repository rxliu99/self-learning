Combine Two Tables

https://leetcode.com/problems/combine-two-tables/
Q1 in https://leetcode.com/list/?selectedList=euurohgj

Takeaways:
	- when to use LEFT JOIN
	
Solution:
SELECT p.firstName,
       p.lastName,
       a.city,
       a.state
FROM Person p
LEFT JOIN Address a
ON p.personId = a.personId;
